# Ingredients Quantity
1 Onion 2
2 Cumin seeds 1/2 teaspoon
3 Green chilli 3
4 Tomato 3
5 Cashew nuts 10 no’s
6 Almonds 10no’s
7 Red chilli powder 1 teaspoon
8 Coriander powder 1 teaspoon
9 Salt to taste
10 Dry fenugreek leaves / kasoori methi 1/2 teaspoon
11 Fresh cream ( Amul ) 1/4 cup
12 Coal Pieces 1 small
13 Butter 1/2 cup
14 Bay leaf 1
15 Food color small pinch

Main Recipe

More Ingredients: Boneless Chicken 1/2 kg, Yogurt 200 gram, Red chilli powder 1 teaspoon, Salt to taste, Ginger-garlic paste 2 teaspoon, Dry fenugreek leaves 1 teaspoon, Lemon juice 2 teaspoon.

Step 1: Take the boneless chicken and cut it into small pieces. Add yogurt, red chilli powder, salt, dry fenugreek leaves, lemon juice and ginger-garlic paste. Mix it well and keep it in a fridge for marinating for at least 6 hours or overnight.

Step 2: Heat the oil in a kadhai or wok and deep fry the chicken pieces till light golden in color or till chicken is completely cooked.

Step 3: Take it out from the oil and keep it aside. Heat the oil in another pan or wok and add chopped onion, cumin seeds and green chilli. Fry it for about 4-5 minutes or till onion is mushy.

Step 4: Add chopped tomato, Cashew nuts, almonds, red chilli powder, coriander powder, dry fenugreek leaves, bay leaves and salt. Cook the spices for another 2-3 minutes. Now let it completely cool by itself.

Step 5: Grind the cooked ingredients along with 1/2 cup water to make it fine and smooth paste.

Step 6: Heat the wok with 1/4 cup butter and add ground paste. Cook the spices on slow heat till butter shows separately. Then add 1/4 cup more butter and food color.

Step 7: Cook it for another 4-5 minutes and add fried chicken pieces and mix it well.

Step 8: Add 2 cup boiled water and cook covered for 5 minutes. Now add fresh cream and mix it well and immediately switch off the flame.

Step 9: Heat the coal pieces on direct flame as shown below in the picture. Make a small bowl from aluminium foil and put it into wok and put the heated coal piece into bowl and pour 1 teaspoon ghee on coal and immediately covered it so that the gravy can absorbs the essence of coal.

Step 10: Butter chicken is ready, garnish with some more butter and serve hot with chapatti or Naan.
